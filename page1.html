Map

Vainglories map consists of a jungle and a lane.

Gameplay

Vainglory is a 3v3 moba(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) where the goal of the game is to farm minions and overpower the opposing team. To win the game, you have to eliminate all of the opponents turrets and destryo their vain crystal.

There are many different heroes to choose from in vainglory but the three main roles are Roam,Jungle, and Carry. In most games, theres usually one of each role although new players may have random team compositions.

Roles

Vainglory sorts each hero into a suggested role (Roam, Jungle,Carry)but some play heroes outside of their suggested role.
