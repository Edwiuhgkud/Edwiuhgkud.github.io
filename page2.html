Heroes

Vainglory consists of 36 different heroes.

Gameplay

The 36 heroes are split into three different roles. Roamers are usually the support of the team. They'll buy items that can heal/protect other team mates while their ablities can stun or heal enemies/allies.

There are two other tyeps of heroes in Vainglory, Jungler and Carry. Junglers are usually melee and assassins while laners are usualy ranged.Both of these classes mainly focus on offensive itmes rather than being team players.

Items

There are multiple different types of items but mainly it's weapon, crystal, defense, consumeables, and support items.
