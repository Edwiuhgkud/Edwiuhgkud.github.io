Items

Vainglory consists of many different items.

Item Overview

Vainglory has many different weapons. The offensive section consists of crystal and weapon items. Defensive items include green and orange items. Green gives health and/or shield(crystal defense). Orange items give armor(weapon defense) or decrease opponents attack speed.

Support items which are purple include boots and other items that have a variety of abilities. Some examples includes an item that can repeat your last used ability or an item that can item silence your opponent. The final section is consumeables. Consumeables can givve vision, boost attack, and even heal but according to the name, they dissapear upon use.

Tips

Vaingloy has many different strategies and can overwhelm new players.
